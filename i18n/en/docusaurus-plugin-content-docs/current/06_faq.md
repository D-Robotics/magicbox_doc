# 6. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1:** What is the reason for the screen freezing and the rocker arm continuously swinging during gesture interaction?  
**A:** Please confirm whether the power adapter being used supports the PD 3.0 protocol.

**Q2:** How to enable Soft AP mode?  
**A:** For instructions on enabling Soft AP mode, refer to [2.1 Network and Bluetooth Configuration](https://developer.d-robotics.cc/rdk_doc/en/System_configuration/network_blueteeth#soft-ap-mode).
